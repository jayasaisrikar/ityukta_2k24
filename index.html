About ITYUKTA

English Meaning: Information
Hindi Meaning: सूचना

ITYUKTA, derived from Sanskrit, embodies the essence of delivering informative content to our readers.

Get ready for an exciting time as ITYUKTA eleventh national-level technical symposium like non other!. ITYUKTA is not just an event; it's a technical fest that brings together the brightest minds with innovative ideas and boundless creativity under one roof. ITYUKTA holds a variety of events, including technical and non-technical challenges, pushing participants to showcase their excellence and problem-solving skills, fostering innovative thinking in line with current trends. Nowadays, the world revolves around Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Deep Learning and web development. To familiarize you with these, we offer workshops like Gemini AI, Web Blaze, and Emotion Recognition Using Facial Analysis to exhilarate you. Our technical events sharpen your mind, encouraging you to unleash your innovation. Our non-tech events offer the most fun and excitement, channeling your energy into thrilling experiences. So, our tech symposium provides you a platform to showcase talents beyond technology, offering two memorable days filled with joy, fun, and excitement.

ITYUKTA MAGAZINE

Welcome to the unveiling of ITYUKTA Magazine, a vibrant encapsulation of the student life within the Information Technology Department at JNTU-GV College of Engineering Vizianagaram(A). Crafted meticulously by the ITYUKTA 2K24 team, this magazine serves as a testament to the dynamic essence of "Student Life in IT". As we prepare to kick off ITYUKTA 2K24, marked by our inaugural ceremony on April 15th, 2024, graced by esteemed Chief Guest Prof. K. Venkatasubbaiah, the magazine stands as a beacon of our collective journey. Through its pages, we traverse the realms of academic excellence, delve into the heart of curricular and co-curricular activities, and celebrate the remarkable achievements of our students. From sporting triumphs to artistic endeavors, ITYUKTA Magazine paints a comprehensive picture of the multifaceted student experience. With a spotlight on the facilities that bolster our academic pursuits, alongside the unwavering support of our faculty, it offers a glimpse into the nurturing environment that defines our department. Beyond the confines of academia, we also amplify student voices, champion women empowerment initiatives, and reminisce on the unforgettable moments from past editions of our fest, including insights from ITYUKTA 2K23. ITYUKTA Magazine is not merely a publication; it is a reflection of our shared ethos, a celebration of our collective spirit, and a testament to the boundless potential of the Information Technology Department at JNTU-GV College of Engineering (A), Vizianagaram. Join us as we embark on this immersive journey through the lens of student life, innovation, and excellence.
